A Collection worth visiting

Showcasing the southwest’s foremost collection of antique and contemporary handmade rugs, Arrediamo Santa Fe specializes in 100% vegetable dyed, hand-spun wool Persian, Afghani, Turkish, Russian, and Tibetan imports.

Choose the Gabbeh rugs from Iran, combining brilliant color palettes, primitive designs, and the artist’s personal style.

Or consider a custom-designed Tufenkian, Tamarian, and New Moon tibetan rugs, directly imported and available in an unlimited selection of sizes and colors.

All rugs are sold with a lifetime exchange policy and certificate of authenticity. Complimentary parking is available.
